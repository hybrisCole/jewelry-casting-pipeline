# Sprue Placement SOP (Manual)

Sprue attachment is **manual in Blender** — not automated via MCP.

## Placement

- Attach at **base center of shank** (thickest cross-section of the band)
- Avoid thin prong tips and fine decorative edges
- Orient for your flask and casting machine (centrifugal vs vacuum)

## Dimensions

| Ring weight (est.) | Sprue diameter |
|--------------------|----------------|
| < 5 g | 3 mm |
| ≥ 5 g | 4 mm |

- Entry angle ~**45°** toward the sprue button / tree gate
- **1 mm fillet** at sprue-to-shank junction (reduces stress, prevents breakage during burnout)

## Before export

- Confirm sprue does not block drain hole if ring is hollow
- Document flask orientation in your print notes
- Export as next checkpoint: `ring_v07_sprued.stl` or final `ring_v06_cast-ready.stl` if sprue is last step
